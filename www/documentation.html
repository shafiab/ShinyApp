Help Guide

The app is pretty straighforward to run. The app shows the EPA National Ambient Air Quality Data for the year 2013. It shows the level of two pollutants - ozone (ppm) and PM2.5 (ug/m3) for a given area within a certain radius.

The main code of the app is taken from the video lecture on yhat. However, I added additional wrapper around the original code, so that instead of entering latitude and longitude as input, the users can just enter zipcode. The app will calculate corresponding latitude and longitude using a lookup table.

Input

The app takes two input:

  1. Zipcode : the default zipcode is 95054. However, users can enter their any zipcode they want.

  2. Radius : the default radius is 40miles. However, users can change the slider to get the desired radius.

Based on the input, the app will calculate the pollutant values and show it in a table.
